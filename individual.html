Individual Sustainability Solutions

This page suggests realistic, cheap, easy ways for individuals like you to be sustainabile in your daily life. Small changes in your day-to-day schedule can go a long way in making the world a cleaner place to live!

Cut down on beef intake
Walk, bike, skate, or run instead of driving
Buy secondhand at thrift stores
Hang clothes to dry instead of using a dryer
Compost food waste
Use reusable grocery bags
Turn of appliances when not in use
Go paper-free!
Use reusable water bottles instead of plastic
Conserve water and take shorter showers
Store leftovers in reusable containers
Xeriscape your lawn to cut down on water usage
